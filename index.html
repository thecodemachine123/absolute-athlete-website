What Is An Athletic Age?

Your chronological age tells you how long you've been alive. Your Athletic Age tells you where you actually are as an athlete — measured through a performance combine that tests the physical foundations every sport is built on.

Strength. Power. Speed. Coordination. Stability. These are the qualities that separate athletes who show up differently. Athletic Age makes them measurable for the first time.

Once we know your Athletic Age, the platform prescribes a personalized training program — engineered specifically to you, not a generic plan — and retests every cycle to show real progress.

The Development Window
AGES 8–14.
THIS IS WHEN
IT HAPPENS.
Ages 8–14 are the period of peak neuromuscular development — when the brain and body are building the movement patterns, coordination, and athletic foundations that carry through an athlete's entire career. What gets built in this window sticks. What gets missed is much harder to recover later. The athletes who develop these foundations before 15 show up differently. Absolute Athlete is built to make sure yours is one of them.
How It Works

MEASURE. PRESCRIBE.
IMPROVE. REPEAT.

A simple three-step cycle that gets smarter with every athlete, every combine, every session.

The Combine

02
The Program

03
The Progress

The Reason Behind AA

Absolute Athlete was founded by Dr. Nathan Price, a chiropractor with 25 years of experience in sports performance, and his two sons Ayden and Andrew. For years, Ayden and Andrew were the test — doing the work in the basement and the driveway during those exact development years. They are both Division I football players now, enjoying the results of that early foundation work.

As a chiropractor, Dr. Price has spent 25 years watching what happens when athletic foundations are built well, and what happens when they are not. He has treated athletes from Olympic gold medalists to Saturday morning rec players, learning something from them all. Absolute Athlete is the early intervention he always wished existed. Individualized, measurable, and accessible to every young athlete — not just the ones with access to private trainers or elite performance programs.

The goal has always been simple: every kid deserves the tools to reach their full athletic potential.

Privacy

PRIVACY POLICY.

Absolute Athlete, Inc. respects the privacy of the families and clubs we work with. This page explains what this website collects and how it is used.

What this site collects

The only information this website collects is what you voluntarily enter into our contact form: your name, email address, club name, and message. We use it solely to respond to your inquiry about Absolute Athlete.

What we don't do

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing. This website does not use advertising trackers, and it does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Athlete data

Athlete accounts and training data live in the Absolute Athlete application, which is separate from this website. Athlete accounts require verified parental consent before activation, and athlete information is never collected through this marketing site.

Service providers

Our contact form is delivered through a third-party form service, and this site is hosted on third-party infrastructure. These providers process your submission only to deliver it to us.

Questions or removal requests

To ask what we hold about you, or to have it deleted, email info@myabsoluteathlete.com.
